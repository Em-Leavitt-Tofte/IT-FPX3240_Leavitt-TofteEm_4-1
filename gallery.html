Listen to “Burn Out to Brilliance”

In 2001, Linda Jones’s world changed. As a young, successful businesswoman, she was constantly on the move, managing a demanding job, traveling, and maintaining a fitness-focused lifestyle. But like many professionals, she ignored early signs of stress and exhaustion, leading to a full collapse. Diagnosed with Chronic Fatigue Syndrome, Linda lost everything.

Refusing to accept that diagnosis as the end of her story, Linda rebuilt her life using a holistic, self-coached approach to healing. Her recovery inspired the founding of the Salus Fatigue Foundation and Life4Changing, which have since helped over 15,000 people reclaim their well-being.

This TEDx talk, “Burn Out to Brilliance: Recovery from Chronic Fatigue”, shares her journey and empowers others to recognize the signs, reset their path, and prioritize sustainable wellness.
